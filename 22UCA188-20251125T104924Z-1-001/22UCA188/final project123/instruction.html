Flight Safety Instructions

Before Takeoff

  • Store your carry-on luggage securely in the overhead compartments or under the seat in front of you.
  • Fasten your seatbelt securely around your hips.
  • Ensure all electronic devices are in airplane mode or turned off as instructed by the crew.

During the Flight

  • Keep your seatbelt fastened whenever you are seated, in case of unexpected turbulence.
  • Follow instructions from the flight crew at all times.
